About Me

My name is Jonathan!

I was born and raised in Florida, the land of oranges, alligators, and the retired. I went to school for chemistry at the University of South Florida and worked as a research assistant at the University of Washington. My worked involved dissecting and extracting salmon tissues to try and determine the cause of pre-spawn mortality, a syndrome that affects salmon that have to migrate through urban streams.

In Washington I was introduced to computer programming, and I ended up getting addicted to the blend of technical challenges and creativity that is involved in creating a full stack application.

Now I'm a Software Engineer in San Francsico looking forward to creating applications that influence peoples lives in a positive way. Feel free to contact me for any reason.
